Want to see your art here?

This is a space for visual storytelling in formats that print cannot support or do justice to. We’re thinking gifs, short films, photojournalism or photo essays, interactive charts and graphics. We will also publish static visual media here as well! If you’re interested in submitting something to our blog, please send an email over to vandhana_ravi@brown.edu.
